Mumbai(Bombay) Mint Centre
If a coin has a "Diamond" under the date of the coin then it shows that the coin ins minted in the Mumbai Mint Centre
Calcutta(Kolkata) Mint Centre
No sign or symbol can be seen below the date of the coin if the coin is minted from Kolkata Mint Centre
Hyderbad Mint Centre
If a coin has a "Star" under the date of the coin then it shows that the coin ins minted in the Hyderabad Mint Centre
Noida Mint Centre
If a coin has a "Dot" under the date of the coin then it shows that the coin ins minted in the Hyderabad Mint Centre
